⚠ BEFORE YOU START — YOU NEED A FREE TAVILY ACCOUNT

KJ2 uses Tavily to search the internet for real results. Without it your agent cannot search the web. It is free and takes two minutes to set up.

Step 1. Open a free account at tavily.com

Step 2. Once signed in you will land on the Overview page. Your API key is shown there — copy it by clicking the two squares icon 📋 next to the key.

Step 3. Keep that key handy — you will paste it into KJ2 when you run it for the first time.

Tavily Usage Note: The free account gives you 1,000 searches per month. Heavy users may need a paid account — prices are in USD. Pay as you go at USD $0.008 per search, or choose a monthly plan for 4,000+ searches. Check current pricing at tavily.com/pricing. For most users the free account is more than enough to start.
OAuth 2.0 — What is it? When KJ2 first opens on your computer it will ask you to log in to Google. This is a one-time secure connection so KJ2 can manage your Gmail and YouTube on your behalf. You click Allow — it never asks again. Your password is never seen or stored by KJ2.
